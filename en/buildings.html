Buildings
There is so much variety in construction: there is the beautiful and the ugly, and probably everything in between. About some, you can't really say anything or form an opinion. Others provoke strong feelings—a building can be incredibly ugly, absolutely magnificent, somehow cozy, or starkly bleak. There are various insights; some smart, some not so much. In our environment, we see timeless solutions alongside passing trends, expensive materials and cheap ones. In this series, you will see various buildings, architectural solutions, and construction in general that have provoked thoughts in me.
