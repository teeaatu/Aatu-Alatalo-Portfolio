Color & Form
In the series *Color & Form*, you can see my observations of the fascinating colors and the shapes they form in our surroundings. Colors have a significant impact on people, and it is a pity that their use has diminished in recent decades.
Color & Form All / Utajärvi / Selected works
Utajärvi is a quiet municipality of about 2,500 inhabitants in North Ostrobothnia, located along the Oulu River. Having always just driven past it on the way to my grandmother's, a sunny summer day finally provided the perfect reason to stop, wander around, and observe the village life. This series documents that day visit and the peaceful summer atmosphere of a small village.
In this section, I have chosen individual works that I personally like. The collection consists of separate observations of the colors in the environment.
