Aatu Alatalo

Photographer Oulu & Hasselblad Masters Finalist

I am a 21-year-old photographer who grew up in the Tyrnävä Lakeus region and currently lives in Oulu. I am studying media at Oulu University of Applied Sciences. I started photography in 2014, and over the past decade, the camera has become an inseparable part of my daily life.

Merits

  • Hasselblad Masters 2026: Finalist in the Project//21 category.
  • SAKUstars 2023: Honorable mention in the "Photographic Art in a Day" series.

Projects

  • Outshine: Digital advertising project on Oulu bus stops in 2025 and 2026.
  • Exhibitions: My works have been exhibited at Piippola Könkkölä and Kestilä Library in 2023 and 2024.
