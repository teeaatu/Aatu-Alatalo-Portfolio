Aatu Alatalo

Photographer based in Oulu & Hasselblad Masters Finalist

I am a 21-year-old photographer raised in the plains of Tyrnävä and currently based in Oulu, Finland. I am studying Visual Communication at the Oulu University of Applied Sciences (OAMK). I picked up the camera in 2014, and over the past decade, photography has become an inseparable part of my daily life.

Achievements

  • Hasselblad Masters 2026: Finalist in the Project // 21 category.
  • SAKUstars 2023: Honorable Mention in the "Photography Art in a Day" category.

Projects

  • Outshine: Digital transit advertising project featured across Oulu bus shelters, 2025 and 2026.
  • Exhibitions: My work has been showcased at Könkkölä in Piippola and the Kestilä Library during 2023 and 2024.
